Roadrunner is our vision to provide a roadmap to the seeker students, a personalized guide to help them navigate to their desired goals. We know how much curious a student is at this point about different directions. And they always tend to search for different careers and roadmaps & requirements for that path.
We want to help them with the roadrunner it serves as a one-way solution to this problem by removing all the major obstacles in their path by navigating the proper personalized roadmap. It consists of different career streams, which appear in the path of a student after their 10th boards exam as per the Indian Education System. Once the student specifies their direction of choice we present to them all the possible career options available in that direction. They can take a look at all of the spots very briefly. Once all their criteria are met with the career option, they can dive deep into that. We have provided every possible information about that option presenting all the pros & cons at the same time, we have given the best possible path to their destination supported by experts in that field.
So, let us dive in.
